Frequently Asked Questions

What shipping options do you offer?+

We offer three shipping partners to give you flexibility: India Post Speed Post (fastest, prepaid only), Shiprocket Express (most affordable, COD available), and Delhivery Premium (most secure, COD available). You can choose your preferred option at checkout based on your needs.

Are your jerseys authentic?+

We offer high-quality replica jerseys with authentic designs. All jerseys feature accurate team colors, logos, and detailing. While these are not official licensed products, they are made with premium materials and craftsmanship.

How long does delivery take?+

Delivery times vary by shipping method: India Post Speed Post takes 4-5 days, Shiprocket Express takes 5-6 days, and Delhivery Premium takes 5-6 days. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days before shipping.

Can I customize my jersey with a name and number?+

Yes! You can add custom names and numbers to most jerseys. Simply specify your customization preferences (name, number, font style) in the order form during checkout. Customization may add 1-2 days to processing time.

What sizes do you offer?+

We offer sizes ranging from S to XXL for most jerseys. Size charts are available on each product page to help you find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Do you accept Cash on Delivery (COD)?+

Yes, COD is available when you choose Shiprocket Express or Delhivery Premium as your shipping method. A COD fee of ₹49 applies. India Post Speed Post is prepaid only.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept online payments through Instamojo (UPI, cards, net banking, wallets) and Cash on Delivery for eligible shipping methods. All online transactions are secure and encrypted.

How do I track my order?+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via Instagram DM or email. You can use this number to track your shipment directly on the courier partner's website.

What is your return policy?+

We accept returns within 48 hours of delivery for wrong products, incorrect sizes, or damaged items. An unboxing video is mandatory for all return claims. Please see our detailed Return Policy page for complete terms and conditions.

How do I contact customer support?+

You can reach us via Instagram DM at @shop.jersivo or @jersivo.community. We typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your message.
