Return, Refund & Exchange Policy

1

Mandatory Unboxing Video Requirement: Customers must record a complete unboxing video where the entire product remains visible at all times. The video must be clear, audible, and show the product from opening to inspection. Returns, refunds, or exchanges will be instantly disqualified if: No unboxing video is provided, the video has partial coverage of the product, the video is blurry or unclear, or the video has no audio.

2

Refund and Exchange Costs: All refund amounts will exclude the original shipping charges paid by the customer. For exchanges, customers will be required to pay shipping charges again for the replacement product delivery.

3

Eligible Cases for Return/Refund/Exchange: Jersivo will only consider return, refund, or exchange requests in the following situations: Wrong product delivered (item does not match the order), incorrect size sent (size different from what was ordered), or product arrived damaged, dirty, or defective.

4

Time Limit for Claims: All return, refund, or exchange requests must be submitted within 48 hours from the time of delivery as confirmed by the delivery partner's tracking system. No requests will be considered after this 48-hour window under any circumstances.
